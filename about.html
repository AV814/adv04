About Me

My name is Alex, and I have been frequently travelling for as long as I can remember. I used to have a trailer with my family at a campsite we would visit every once in a while, mostly in the summer. In addition to that, my family would take yearly visits to my grandparent's condo in Naples, Florida. Every year my Dad and I have taken what we call "Man-cations", where we do a different historical trip every summer. I would say this is the main thing that has gotten me to love doing this sort of thing often.
